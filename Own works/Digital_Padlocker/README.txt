MATERIALS
 - LCD screen 16x2
 - Servomotor
 - Potentiometer
 - Passive buzzer
 - IR module
 - IR control
 - 2x R-330 Ohms
 - 1x R-10 KOhms
 - Green led
 - Red led
 - 2x buttons
 - Photoresistor

FUNCTIONALITY
 - Simulate a digital padlocker with a screen displaying instructions and values. There are events depending on the step you are at. These events came from the led´s, 
   the buzzer, the screen and the servomotor (when the password is correct it turns 90º). There is also a reset password combination: press the two buttons and apply a high light value to the photo resistor
